Web Development

My expertise is front end development. I have creative coding skills, an eye for design, and the communications experience to help guide projects from concept to execution.

What I do

During my time at Inviqa, I've developed sites for international brands including Acne Studios, PZ Cussons, and Nokia Solutions and Networks.

Where I’ve worked

Singing

I am a versatile soprano. I sing primarily with small acappella ensembles and have studied performance at a postgraduate level. My tastes are diverse and include Renaissance polyphony, folk, and modern compositions. I have been on tours within North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia / New Zealand.

Who I’ve sung with

I have been a member of various ensembles including the National Youth Choir of Canada, Thames Scholars Summer School, the Ebor Singers, the 24, and the Vancouver Cantata Singers. I’ve studied privately with teachers including tenors Torin Chiles and John Potter, and sopranos Lynne Dawson and Janet Coxwell.

Where I’ve studied

After attending Cardinal Carter Academy for the Arts in Toronto, I studied theory and early music at the University of Western Ontario and received a BMus (Honors) in 2006. In 2008, I received an MA in Music from the University of York in the United Kingdom where I studied Vocal Studies with John Potter.
