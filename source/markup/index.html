I have over a decade of experience in professional front end web development. I am charismatic and as a technical team lead I works to support my team in line with the requirements of my clients.

What I do

I am passionate about front end development with an emphasis on the user experience. I prioritise intuitive web design using atomic design principles and style guide driven development, with an emphasis on usability, accessibility, and brand identity. I've has worked on projects that support multiple languages and themes for international audiences.

Where I work

In 2013 I joined Inviqa’s Sheffield office and I currently am a technical team lead and senior front end developer. I primarily work on PHP sites (Magento 1 and 2, Drupal, EZ Platform), working closely with back end developers, designers, testers, and our clients in an Agile environment. During my time at Inviqa, I’ve worked on sites for international brands such as graze.com, Nokia Networks, Warner Music, and various fashion brands including Missguided and Acne Studios.

Where I’ve worked before

Previously, I’ve had roles with organizations in Canada and the UK including Mountain Equipment Co-op, Vancity Credit Union, and the University of York. I’ve also done some freelance work but I really prefer being part of a team.
